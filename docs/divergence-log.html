Divergence Log

The site's namesake: every completed match, scored against all three sources. Where did the statistical model, the sportsbook consensus, and Polymarket agree — and where did they diverge? For each result we show what each source put on the outcome that actually happened, the model-vs-market gap, and which source called it.

Over 88 scored matches: no-credit verdicts — markets agreed 6 · sources agreed 30 · all missed 29.

A "distinct verdict win" means that source put a clearly higher probability (> 2pp) on the actual outcome than every other source. When the two markets jointly beat the model they share a no-credit "markets agreed" verdict — neither book nor Polymarket is credited individually, so a market's win count never double-counts. Brier and log loss score every source on every match where it posted a price (lower is better), independent of the verdict. See the methodology and calibration page.
